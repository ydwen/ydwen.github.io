Biography

I am a first year Ph.D student at CMU, advised by Prof. Bhiksha Raj and Prof. Ming Li.

Before coming CMU, I was a visiting student in SIAT, advised by Prof. Zhifeng Li and Prof. Yu Qiao.

I got my M. Eng degree from School of Electronic and Information Engineering, South China University of Technology in 2016, where I received the B. Eng degree in 2013. My supervisor is Prof. Yuli Fu.

My research interest mainly focus on deep learning and its applications on computer vision, especially face recognition. I am so lucky to work closely with Weiyang Liu, Kaipeng Zhang, Zhiding Yu, Prof. Meng Yang.
