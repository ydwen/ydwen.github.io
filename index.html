Biography (CV)

I am currently a final-year M.S. candidate with School of Electronic and Information Engineering, South China University of Technology, China, where I obtained my bachelor degree.

 

My research interest mainly lies in computer vision, deep learning, sparse coding and face recognition.

 

I am so lucky to work closely with Weiyang Liu, Prof. Meng Yang. I am advised by Prof. Yuli Fu in SCUT and co-advised by Prof. Prof. Zhifeng Li and Prof. Yu Qiao in SIAT.
