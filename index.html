About Me

I am currently a tenure-track assistant professor at Westlake University. My research focuses on large language model, vision language model and finetuning techinique.

Before joining Westlake University, a post-doctoral researcher at MPI Tübingen, working with Michael J. Black. I completed my PhD at Carnegie Mellon University, where I was advised by Prof. Rita Singh and Bhiksha Raj. I was a member of MLSP Group. Prior to that, I received my master's and bachelor's degrees from South China University of Technology, advised by Yuli Fu. I have also spent time at Facebook Reality Lab and Shenzhen Institute of Advanced Technology.
